*tools: [[Unity]], [[Cluster]]*

An experimental world that allows interplanetary travel. It is unlike other game worlds that are "space themed" since the ship can truly move freely in 3D space and the planets are procedurally generated. I wanted to replicate the magic of No Mans Sky in a way that felt more tangible, like watching someone else drive the ship in the cockpit while you lounge in the hub.
