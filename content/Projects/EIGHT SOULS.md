An abandoned action RPG prototype for Cluster. Its goal was to provide cooperative gameplay that makes people rely on each other and provide player expression that goes beyond just the looks of their avatar (like, how cool would it be if I had magic and resisted fire and you bled blue and breathed underwater??).
